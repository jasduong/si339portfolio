👋 hi there!

my name is jasmine duong. i am a student at the university of michigan school of information. i'm studying information science with a concentration in UX design. i'm minoring in law, justice, and social change at the college of literature, science, and arts at UMICH.

as a UX/UI designer, i love opportunities where I can critically think about how technology may enhance human experience.

💭 why ux?

throughout high school, i loved participating in community service activities such as volunteering at my town's local hospital with dreams of becoming a pediatric physician's assistant (PA) when i grew older. however, as a self-taught artist, my passion for creativity, curiosity, and service eventually led me towards the field of user experience. in this field, a marriage between work infused with empathy and artistic creation is able to blossom. i hope to provide solutions to complex human issues through qualitative and quantitative processes.

💒 my ux mission

to use technology and art to bring positive change that allows for inclusive, accessible, and (of course!) beautiful experiences!

💌 my guiding interests

immigration rights, disability justice, first generation / lgbtq+ youth, poc solidarity, women's rights and all intersections in between
